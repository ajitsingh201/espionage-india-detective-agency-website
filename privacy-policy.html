Privacy Policy

Your trust is our foundation. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining absolute confidentiality throughout our investigation services.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Espionage India, we understand that privacy is not just a policy—it's a promise. As a leading private detective agency, we handle some of the most sensitive and confidential information imaginable. This privacy policy outlines how we protect your data, maintain confidentiality, and ensure your trust remains unbroken.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

During our professional relationship, we may collect various types of information necessary to conduct thorough investigations while maintaining legal compliance:

  • Personal Information: Names, contact details, addresses, and identification documents provided by clients
  • Case-Related Information: Details about your situation, concerns, and investigation objectives
  • Evidence and Documentation: Photographs, videos, audio recordings, and written reports generated during investigations
  • Communication Records: Emails, phone calls, messages, and meeting notes related to your case
  • Financial Information: Payment details and billing information for service transactions

How We Collect Information

We gather information through various legitimate channels:

  1. Direct Communication: Information you provide during consultations, emails, phone calls, and written correspondence
  2. Investigation Activities: Evidence collected through surveillance, background checks, and field investigations
  3. Public Records: Information legally obtained from government databases, court records, and public directories
  4. Third-Party Sources: Verified information from legitimate sources with proper authorization when required

Important: We never collect information through illegal means, unauthorized access, or unethical practices. All data collection complies with Indian laws and regulations.

How We Use Your Information

Your information serves specific, legitimate purposes essential to our professional services:

  • Case Investigation: Conducting thorough investigations based on your objectives and concerns
  • Evidence Preparation: Compiling reports, documentation, and evidence for legal proceedings
  • Client Communication: Keeping you informed about case progress and developments
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and maintaining professional standards
  • Quality Assurance: Reviewing and improving our services while maintaining anonymity

Data Protection Measures

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

Physical Security

  • Secure office premises with access control systems
  • Locked filing cabinets for physical documents
  • Restricted access to investigation materials
  • Confidential meeting rooms for client discussions

Digital Security

  • Encrypted communication channels for sensitive information
  • Password-protected databases with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security audits and system updates
  • Secure cloud storage with end-to-end encryption

Personnel Security

  • Background-checked and trained investigators
  • Confidentiality agreements with all team members
  • Regular privacy training and awareness programs
  • Limited access to case information on need-to-know basis

Confidentiality Guarantee

Our confidentiality commitment extends beyond legal requirements:

  • Absolute Discretion: We never disclose client identity or case details without explicit written consent
  • Anonymous Operations: Our investigators work discreetly without revealing client information
  • Secure Communications: All discussions and correspondence remain confidential
  • Post-Case Protection: Confidentiality continues indefinitely after case completion

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain strict limits on information sharing:

We DO NOT share your information with:

  • Marketing companies or advertisers
  • Media organizations or journalists
  • Unrelated third parties
  • Social media platforms

We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or court order
  • Explicitly authorized by you in writing
  • Necessary for legal proceedings with your consent
  • Protecting our legal rights with minimal disclosure

Data Retention Policy

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Cases: Information maintained throughout the investigation period
  • Completed Cases: Records retained for legal requirements (typically 7 years)
  • Digital Evidence: Securely archived with restricted access
  • Deletion Requests: Honored when legally permissible

Upon request, we can provide secure destruction certificates for sensitive documents and digital files after the retention period.

Your Rights and Choices

You maintain control over your information:

  • Access Rights: Request copies of your personal information in our possession
  • Correction Rights: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion Rights: Request deletion when legally permissible
  • Restriction Rights: Limit how we use your information
  • Consent Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for specific uses

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses minimal tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us improve website performance (anonymous data only)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use advertising or marketing cookies
  • Cookie Control: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser

Third-Party Services

We carefully vet all third-party services:

  • Payment processors with PCI-DSS compliance
  • Cloud storage providers with encryption standards
  • Communication platforms with end-to-end encryption
  • Background check services with legal compliance

International Data Transfers

As an India-based agency:

  • All data remains within Indian jurisdiction unless legally required otherwise
  • We comply with Indian data protection laws and regulations
  • Cross-border transfers only occur with explicit client consent
  • International cases handled through secure, encrypted channels

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental consent. Investigations involving minors require legal guardian authorization and comply with child protection laws.

Changes to This Policy

We update our privacy policy periodically:

  • Significant changes will be communicated to active clients
  • Policy updates posted on our website
  • Last updated date clearly indicated
  • Continued use of services implies acceptance of updates
